Who We Are

Engineering Water Solutions Since 1999

Mangalam HDPE Pipes is a leading manufacturer of high-density polyethylene piping systems, serving municipal corporations, government projects, and private industries across India with uncompromising quality and reliability.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Pune produces pipes conforming to IS 4984, IS 14333, and international standards — backed by a dedicated R&D team and stringent quality control processes.
